About Dune: Imperium

Dune: Imperium is a competitive strategy game for 2-4 players set in the iconic Dune universe created by Frank Herbert. Players take on the roles of the noble Houses of the Imperium, vying for control of the desert planet Arrakis, the only known source of the valuable spice Melange.

The game combines elements of deckbuilding, worker placement, and area control to create a unique and engaging experience. Players must carefully manage their resources, forge alliances, and outmaneuver their opponents to secure their victory.

Gameplay

  • Players take turns placing workers on the game board to collect resources, build their armies, and gain political influence.
  • Resources can be used to purchase cards that provide special abilities or bonuses.
  • Political influence can be used to control the Landsraad, the galactic parliament, and pass laws that benefit your House.
  • Military units can be used to conquer territories on Arrakis and gain control of the spice trade.
  • The game ends when one House has achieved victory by controlling a majority of the Landsraad or conquering a certain number of territories.
